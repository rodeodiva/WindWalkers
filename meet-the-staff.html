Gabrielle Greeves – Executive Director– Gabrielle is a recognized veteran in the field of non-profits who brings 20+ years of experience in youth development, fundraising, administration, marketing and finance to WindWalkers.  She offers a strong track record in consulting on variety of issues. She is a former principle on the MAN UP Campaign that create a strong platform for eradicating violence against women- giving voice to the voiceless- in 24 countries. Gabrielle supports the daily administration, grant writing, fund raising events, public relations and name exposure on behalf of WindWalkers.

Beth Gusick – Program Director and PATH, Intl Certified Instructor– Beth began riding at 6 and rode as a Pony Club member until she was 21. In 1995 she spent the summer working at a therapeutic riding center and has been hooked on therapeutic riding ever since! She became a PATH (Professional Association of Therapeutic Horsemanship registered instructor in 1996 and an PATH Advanced Instructor in 2002. She began her career at the Colorado Therapeutic Riding Center (CTRC) in Boulder where she worked for 5 years; then she moved to Jackson Hole, Wyoming where she worked for a small, growing center for 7 years. Beth returned to CTRC in 2006 where she was the Program Coordinator and Head of Instructors. Beth has been with WindWalkers since September of 2008 and has aided us in becoming a highly regarded therapeutic center.

Shannon Weeks – Graphic Designer– Shannon has been a volunteer for our organization for over 4 years. She is a well known member of the Carbondale community, and competes in local rodeos. Shannon is currently studying Marketing at Colorado Mesa University. She hopes to help WindWalkers grow as a strong organization in the Roaring Fork Valley. She aids Gabrielle in creating advertising and marketing campaigns throughout the year.
